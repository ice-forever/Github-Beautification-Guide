Moe-counter [萌萌计数器]

一个多种风格可选的萌萌计数器，可用于网站访问统计等

Demo:https://count.getloli.com

如何使用:(NAME为与计数数据对应的字符串)

SVG address:

https://count.getloli.com/get/@NAME

Img tag:

<img src="https://count.getloli.com/get/@NAME" alt="NAME" />

Markdown:

![:name](https://count.getloli.com/get/@NAME)


本网站访问计数


更多主题:

只需使用查询参数theme，例如: https://count.getloli.com/get/@NAME?theme=moebooru
